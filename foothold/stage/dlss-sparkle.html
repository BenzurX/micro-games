DLSS + Frame Gen — sparkle options

Four alternatives for the gag "graphics enhancement" effect. Each preview runs the real Phaser particle config over a faux board, so whatever you pick lifts straight into startDlssSparkles() in src/lib/ui.js. Tell me the letter and I'll wire it in.

A Twinkle Stars

Small gold/white 4-point stars twinkling in place, sparse and tasteful. A calmer, less rainbow take on what's in the build now.

B Anamorphic Flares

Bright cyan/white points with a horizontal light streak — reads like a lens flare / "graphics card" glint. Fewer, bigger, more premium-tech.

C Holo Bokeh Rising

Soft rainbow orbs drifting slowly upward, fading as they go. Dreamy, holographic, ambient — least "in your face".

D Prismatic Bursts

Little firework pops of rainbow shards firing off at random spots. The most celebratory / triumphant, matches the fanfare energy.

NOW Star (rainbow)

The current build for reference: multicolored rotating 4-point stars, everywhere at once.

How they differ. A & NOW sit still (twinkle); B is sparse bright streaks; C drifts upward; D bursts radially. All use additive blending so they glow over the dark board.

Tuning. Density (frequency), size (scale), and palette (tint) are all easy to dial after you pick a direction — e.g. "B, but a touch denser".
