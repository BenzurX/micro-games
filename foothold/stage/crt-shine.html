CRT glass reflection - options

Four takes on the "light in the room reflecting off the glass" highlight, plus the current faint glow for reference. Each preview runs the real CRT shader (same warp, scanlines, grille, vignette as the game) with a different shine block, so whatever you pick lifts straight into CrtPipeline.js. All sit up near the top, where a ceiling light or window would actually reflect on a screen. Tell me the letter and I'll wire it in.

A Window Pane

A soft-edged rounded rectangle up in the top-left, like a bright window reflected in the glass. The most literal "there's a window in the room" read.

B Ceiling Spotlight

A small hot core with a wide soft halo, top-center-left - a ceiling bulb or downlight glinting off the curved glass. Bright and focal.

C Fluorescent Streak

A long angled bar sweeping across the glass, like a strip light or tube reflected on the diagonal. The most dynamic / directional.

D Twin Lights + Sheen

Two offset soft blobs (two ceiling lights) over a faint broad brightening toward the top. The busiest and most "real room" of the set.

NOW Current glow

What's in the build today: a single very faint radial glow low-left. Here for comparison - this is the one you found too subtle.

Orientation note. In this shader y increases upward, so all four reflections are placed high on the screen (y ~0.75-0.86) where room light really lands on a tube. The current glow sits low because its center was authored just below the bottom edge.

Tuning. Brightness, size, position, and tint (kept a cool near-white) are all one-line tweaks after you pick - e.g. "B, but half the brightness and nudged to the corner".
