CRT window reflection - crooked variants

Four more distorted / crooked takes on the Window Pane reflection, all at about half the previous brightness (0.24 → 0.12). Same real CRT shader as the game; whatever you pick lifts straight into CrtPipeline.js. The last card shows the plain upright pane at the new reduced brightness as a baseline.

A Tilted Pane

The whole window rotated off-axis, like the screen is angled to the window. Clean rectangle, just leaning.

B Leaning Parallelogram

Sheared into a parallelogram and tilted - a stronger perspective lean, as if the window is off to the side of the room.

C Warped Glass

Rectangle with wavy, rippling edges, like an old imperfect CRT curving the reflection. The most irregular / organic.

D Tilted + Mullions

Tilted pane with a cross bar dividing it into four lights - unmistakably a window, crooked on the glass.

FLAT Upright baseline

The original upright pane at the new reduced brightness, for comparison against the crooked ones above.

Tuning. Tilt angle, skew amount, ripple strength, size, position, and brightness are all one-line tweaks after you pick - e.g. "C, but tilt it more and nudge it left".
