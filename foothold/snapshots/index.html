v0.05 CURRENT July 6, 2026
Upgrade economy, a CRT filter & a mobile-first responsive layout
  • Fortify is now Upgrade: spend stone to double a resource node’s output instead of walling it; captured nodes arrive un-upgraded
  • New CRT post-processing filter (first pass): a subtle barrel curve, scanlines, phosphor grille, soft bloom & a gentle vignette — toggleable in CrtPipeline.js
  • Mobile-first responsive height: the board fills tall phones instead of letterboxing, with the HUD pinned top and the legend + End Turn pinned to the bottom
  • Board now vertically centers in the space between the top and bottom zones on any device aspect ratio
  • Fixed a double-centering bug that pushed the canvas off toward the lower-right

v0.04 July 4, 2026
Game-feel & juice pass — sound, particles, shake
  • Procedural Web Audio SFX (no sample files): a distinct sound on claim, build, fortify, siege, end-turn, win & lose
  • Every sound has 5 slight pitch variants so rapid repeats don’t fatigue the ear; fortify is a metallic anvil “tang”
  • Capturing the enemy home plays its own heavier, pitched-down sound (not a normal siege)
  • Colored particle bursts on each tile that flips, plus camera shake (small on siege, big on a base capture)
  • Tiles pop in with an overshoot; the AI now plays its moves one at a time so each lands; animated win/lose overlay
  • Win banner now reads “You Win on Round N!”; fortify strengthened to ×3 defense

v0.03 July 3, 2026
Portrait 6×9 board, a river to cross & real icons
  • Board reshaped to 6 wide × 9 tall (portrait) for one-handed mobile play
  • A meandering river splits the board through the middle rows — impassable and unclaimable, one water tile per column
  • Exactly two bridges (one per half) are the only claimable, passable crossings
  • Balanced node economy per half (2 wood / 2 gold / 2 stone) with asymmetric positions and 2 contested specials by the water
  • Kenney “Board Game Icons” (CC0), tinted per resource, replace the placeholder squares; hover tooltips explain each tile

v0.02 July 2, 2026
New action model, resource split & base-capture win
  • Four actions: Claim (empty, gold), Build (resource tile, wood), Fortify (own node, stone), Siege (enemy tile, gold; 2× if fortified)
  • One job per resource; player now bottom-right (blue) vs AI top-left (red)
  • HUD reworked: resource counters with per-turn income (+N) and a color-key action legend
  • Filled purple shields mark fortified tiles; home tiles can't be fortified
  • Win by capturing the enemy base (instant) or holding the most tiles at round 12
